## Transportation To/From <CITY>

### By Air  

To get to Memorial University from St. John's International Airport (YYT):  

* Public transit:
The St. John’s Transportation Commission (Metrobus) provides a public transit service to and from the airport to popular destinations in the St. John’s metro region, including Memorial University. **Route 14** travels between the airport and MUN campus. As an accessible facility, the Airport is pleased that Metrobus has committed to providing a wheelchair accessible service on this route. Metrobus can also accommodate large, non-folding and non-collapsible mobility aids.

The schedule for Route 14 can be found [here](https://www.metrobus.com/schedules/fall/route14/). 

More details on fares and passes can be found [here](https://www.metrobus.com/fares/).



* Taxi service:
St. John’s International Airport currently operates a Closed Taxi Stand. City Wide Taxi is the exclusive provider of ground transportation services for the public at St. John’s International Airport. Taxi cabs queue outside the Arrivals Area. A taxi dispatcher is available to assist you.

Taxi rates are set for hotels and B&Bs. Because taxis are waiting for you upon arrival, a premium rate is set so that customers can avail of immediate service. Please refer to the [YYT website](https://stjohnsairport.com/to-from-airport/ground-transportation/taxis/) for more information about fares.


### By Car
This will of course depend on where you are driving from. [Parking information is available here.](https://www.mun.ca/parking/)

There is a parking garage north of the CSF with pay-per-use- and permit-parking spots. If you would like to request a visitor permit, please email [atlantic@bioinformatics.ca](mailto:atlantic@bioinformatics.ca) for more information.
