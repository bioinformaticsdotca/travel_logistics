## Transportation To/From Winnipeg

### By Air  

Winnipeg Richardson International Airport (YWG)
YWG is located 9.2 km west of downtown Winnipeg. 

* Public transit: [Winnipeg Transit’s Route 224](https://www.ywg.ca/en/passengers/transportation/public-transit/) bus runs from YWG to University of Manitoba, Bannatyne Campus. Once it reaches Westbound Tecumseh at Tecumseh, you can walk towards William Avenue around 300 m to reach the University Campus. 

* Limousine/Taxi service: YWG has several taxi partner companies. [You can find more information here](https://www.ywg.ca/en/passengers/transportation/taxis/).
